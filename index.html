Workflow

How it works

From market scan to on-chain execution in four disciplined steps.

01

Scan for Proven Winners

The engine ingests thousands of bettor profiles and filters for statistical edge: high volume, high win rate, proven profit factor, and p-value significance.

02

Run Monte Carlo EV

Every candidate signal is stress-tested with 10,000 stochastic simulations. Expected value, variance, and tail risk are quantified before a dollar is committed.

03

Size with Kelly Criterion

Position size is computed from edge and bankroll. Fractional Kelly (0.25x–0.5x) prevents over-betting while capturing convex upside.

04

Execute with Risk Guards

The trade hits the CLOB only if all 6 circuit breakers are green. Real-time monitoring continues until resolution or manual halt.
